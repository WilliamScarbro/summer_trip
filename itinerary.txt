SUMMER ROAD TRIP ITINERARY
Fort Collins, CO to Eugene, OR
Dates: Friday 2026-05-15 to Monday 2026-06-22

Planning assumptions
- Drive times are route estimates for normal conditions. Long-haul legs are based on current Travelmath route estimates; shorter local days are rounded estimates.
- Total projected driving: about 2,700 miles.
- Gas projection: about $475 at 25 mpg and a blended $4.40/gal average across CO/UT/NV/CA/OR.
- Gas sensitivity at 25 mpg: about $432 at $4.00/gal, about $454 at $4.20/gal, about $475 at $4.40/gal, about $497 at $4.60/gal.
- Lodging: friend stays and van camping.
- Free-camp guidance is area-level only. Check local MVUMs, seasonal closures, and posted stay limits before you park.
- River days are flow-dependent. Re-check American Whitewater and local gauges the week of each run.
- Klamath Falls is the southern Oregon river stop.
- Hot spring stops use undeveloped or unimproved sites from the USGS hot springs layer.

Permit notes
- Diamond Peak Wilderness requires a free self-issue permit for day use and overnight stays from June 15 through October 15.
- Waldo Lake Wilderness requires a self-issue permit between Memorial Day and October 31.

Rivers to target
- South Fork American near Coloma, California: use the Chili Bar / Route 193 to Coloma section tied to AW’s Chili Bar gauge
- Upper Klamath / Keno area near Klamath Falls, Oregon: Keno Run
- Middle Fork Willamette near Jasper, Oregon: use the Dexter Dam to Coast Fork confluence section tied to AW’s Jasper gauge
- McKenzie, Oregon: use AW’s Vida-linked sections, especially Finn Rock to Leaburg and Frissell to Finn Rock
- North Umpqua near Glide, Oregon: use the lower section around Idleyld Park and Glide

Hot springs to fold in
- Big Bend or Hunt Hot Spring in the Pit River country on June 2
- McCredie Hot Springs on June 11 before Bend

Free-camp zones to lean on
- Uinta-Wasatch-Cache NF around Park City
- Humboldt-Toiyabe NF or nearby BLM outside Elko
- Tahoe NF and Eldorado NF on the Sierra leg
- Shasta-Trinity NF north of San Francisco
- Fremont-Winema NF and the Klamath Basin around Klamath Falls
- Umpqua NF around Mt. Thielsen and the North Umpqua corridor
- Willamette NF in the Diamond Peak, Waldo, McKenzie, Oakridge, and Jasper corridors
- Deschutes NF outside Bend
